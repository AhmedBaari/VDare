About Our Innovation

Orthopedic disorders, particularly osteoarthritis (OA), are among the leading causes of disability worldwide. Early detection is crucial for effective management, but conventional diagnostic methods such as X-rays and MRIs are expensive, time-consuming, and not always effective in detecting early-stage conditions.

VDARE TECHSOLUTIONS (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED aims to develop and commercialize an IoT-based AI-driven thermal imaging device that enables non-invasive, real-time detection of OA. By integrating infrared thermography (IRT) with AI analytics, we provide a portable, user-friendly, and cost-effective solution for healthcare professionals and telemedicine applications.

Founded in April 2025, we are a technology startup focused on computer programming, consultancy, and related activities in the healthcare sector. Our mission is to revolutionize early detection of orthopedic conditions through innovative technology.
